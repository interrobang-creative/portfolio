Vancouver Spirits

This project is an exploration of what an esports branch of the Vancouver Grizzlies might have looked like. The branding is inspired by the Kermode bear, also known as the Spirit Bear, the official provincial mammal of British Columbia. My goal was to create a brand that pays homage to the aesthetic Vancouver sports teams have established, while doing something new in the esports space.
