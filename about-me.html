About Me

Since graduating from Capilano University's IDEA program in 2015, I've been an active part of the esports industry here in Vancouver. I've always been a gamer at heart, and that passion has led me to contribute to the community at every level, whether that's helping individual streamers, designing tournaments, or branding organizations. My goal is to help grow and legitimize the esports industry, giving it the production quality it needs to inspire the minds of players, fans and sponsors.
